Attack On Saif Ali Khan | Stabbed 6 Times | Is He Safe?


Key Takeaways:

  • Saif Ali Khan was Stabbed Six Times During a Burglary Attempt at His Home.

  • The actor is now out of danger following surgery and is recovering at Lilavati Hospital.

  • Mumbai Police report the assailant entered the residence through the fire escape.

Saif Ali Khan was stabbed 6 times in his home during a burglary attempt. He is recovering after surgery at Lilavati Hospital. Mumbai Police say the attacker entered through a fire escape.

The incident occurred around 2:30 AM, Saif tried to intervene, which led to the stabbing. His wife, Kareena Kapoor, and their sons were home at the time.

A statement from Saif's team requested patience from the media and fans. They confirmed the burglary attempt and his surgery.

Dr. Nitin Dange, who performed the surgery, reported deep and superficial wounds, a knife was lodged in Saif's spine but has been removed. He is now stable and out of danger.

The attack has raised concerns about law and order in Mumbai. Political leaders are now criticizing it. Priyanka Chaturvedi questioned the police's effectiveness. Clyde Crasto expressed fear for common citizens' safety.

What’s People Take On Saif Ali Khan Attack?

Saif Ali Khan's attack raises serious concerns about normal people's safety, if strong, high-profile individuals can be attacked in their homes, what hope is there for ordinary citizens? Crasto expressed this worry in a post on X. 

He noted that the fear of the law seems to be waning in Maharashtra. This decline is due to leniency over the past few years.

NCP (SP) leader Clyde Crasto also questioned the law and order situation in Mumbai, saying that "fear of law seems to be at a low in Maharashtra due to leniencies in the past couple of years."
